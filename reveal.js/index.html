Lessons Learned

  • Stop being prideful
  • Go out there and meet people
  • Everyone you meet is a potential lead
  • Nothing is given to you, everything is earned
  • You have to pay for your mistakes
  • You can't wait for things to happen, you have to make them happen yourself

Next Steps

  • Disliked my job
  • Found another job through a lead
  • Got laid off (blessing in disguise)
  • Decided to get back into programming
  • Found out about Web Development Fellowship program being sponsored by the city
  • Got accepted!

Finished the program. back to interview world

  • Continued to follow the lessons learned after college
  • Had many potential leads
  • Did not cold apply to places
  • Kept attending meetups and meeting people
  • Kept working towards the goal of getting a job instead of waiting
  • Met my future boss months prior without even knowing it

Wizard Development

  • Had leads

We really like you, your super passionate and would be a great addition to our team but ...

  • We don't have a position for you
  • We don't have the proper resources/mentorship for you
  • We urge you to reapply in 2016
  • You are not senior enough

We are looking for someone who can come in and make miracles

Started to

  • Ask for feedback
  • Do mock interviews
  • Whiteboarding
  • Going over tutorials
  • Practicing under pressured environments
  • Work on side projects
  • Show more interest in the company than the technology
  • Believe more in myself

The most amazing thing started to happen

After accepting one I felt so relieved

Recap of important lessons learned

  • Know What you want to do
  • Dev and Design world is too small, do not burn bridges
  • Make friends not contacts
  • Work and reach out your network
  • Don't be Afraid to Ask Questions
  • Believe in yourself
  • Prepare Yourself Mentally
  • Interview sooner than you think
  • Never stop practicing, always work on your craft
  • Continue learning
